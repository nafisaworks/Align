Posture Guide

🪑 Sitting Posture

Keep your back straight, shoulders relaxed, and screen at eye level. Avoid slouching or leaning forward.

🧍 Standing Posture

Stand tall with shoulders back, weight evenly distributed, and head aligned with your spine.

❌ Common Mistakes

Slouching, uneven shoulders, leaning to one side, and forward head posture.

💡 Tips

Take breaks, stretch regularly, and maintain awareness of your posture.
