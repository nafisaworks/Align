Posture & Biomechanics Guide

🪑 Sitting Posture

Keep your spine neutral, shoulders relaxed, and feet flat on the ground. Your screen should be at eye level to avoid forward head posture.

🧍 Standing Posture

Your ears, shoulders, hips, and knees should form a straight vertical line. Weight should be evenly distributed on both feet.

📐 Biomechanics Insight

In ideal posture:

• Head aligns vertically above shoulders
• Shoulder angle remains balanced
• Spine maintains natural curvature
• Knee joints align under hips

Small deviations can increase strain on muscles and joints over time.

❌ Common Mistakes

• Slouching forward
• Uneven shoulders
• Leaning to one side
• Forward head posture

💡 Tips for Improvement

• Take breaks every 30–60 minutes
• Stretch your back and shoulders
• Adjust chair and screen height
• Stay mindful of your posture
