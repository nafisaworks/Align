About ALIGN

ALIGN is an interactive web-based application designed to help users improve their posture through real-time analysis and feedback. By combining principles of biomechanics with computer vision, the platform detects body alignment and provides meaningful suggestions to correct posture in both sitting and standing positions.

The goal of ALIGN is to address a common real-world problem—poor posture—by making awareness and correction accessible, engaging, and easy to understand. Alongside the live posture analyzer, the platform also offers educational content to help users understand the science behind proper alignment and movement.

This project demonstrates the integration of technology, health awareness, and user-centered design to create a practical and impactful solution.
