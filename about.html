About ALIGN

ALIGN is an interactive web-based platform designed to help users improve their posture through real-time analysis and feedback. By combining biomechanics concepts with computer vision, it detects body alignment and provides meaningful suggestions for both sitting and standing positions.

The aim of ALIGN is to solve a real-world problem—poor posture—by making awareness and correction simple, accessible, and engaging. Alongside live analysis, the platform also educates users about proper posture and movement.
